Two functions $\color{red}{f},\color{blue}{g} : X\to Y$ are said to be \textbf{\textit{homotopic}} if there is a homotopy $h_t : X\to Y$ such that $h_0=\color{red}{f}$ and $h_1=\color{blue}{g}$, and we write $f\simeq g$.
\end{center}
